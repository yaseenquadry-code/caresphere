Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

CareSphere collects personal and health information you provide when registering, booking appointments, purchasing medicines, or using our telemedicine services. This includes name, contact details, date of birth, medical history, prescriptions, and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide and improve our healthcare services, process payments, send appointment confirmations, and enable doctor-patient consultations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest. We are HIPAA compliant and ISO 27001 certified. Our infrastructure undergoes regular security audits and penetration testing.

4. Health Data

Medical records, prescriptions, and diagnostic reports are stored with highest-level encryption. Only you and your authorized healthcare providers can access this information. Doctors can only access records relevant to your consultation.

5. Payment Information

We do not store your payment card details. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment processors. QR and UPI payments are handled by RBI-regulated payment gateways.

6. Refund Policy

Appointment cancellations made 24+ hours before the scheduled time receive a 100% refund within 3-5 business days. Same-day cancellations receive a 50% refund. Medicine orders can be cancelled before dispatch for a full refund. Lab test bookings can be cancelled up to 2 hours before the sample collection time for a full refund.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. Analytics cookies help us improve our services. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@caresphere.in to exercise these rights.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@caresphere.in or call 1800-111-555.
